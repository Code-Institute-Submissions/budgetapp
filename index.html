Budget app

Visualize your income, and split your earnings into common expenses and savings goals to help you make the most out of your money.

How to use the monthly budget calculator?

  1. Enter the total budget into the Budget fields that apply to you or your family, then click on the "set budget" button.
  2. In the Expenses section, add the product name or category as you wish to classify them. Add the expenses as accurately as possible. If you don't have the exact number, try to put an estimated amount. Once you have the product name and amount, click on the "Check amount" button.
  3. You will see how the budget is added and the expenses are automatically added up, generating a balance in the next section.
  4. The list of expenses will also be added at the bottom of the calculator, where you can modify (pencil) or delete (trash can) any of your expenses if you wish.
